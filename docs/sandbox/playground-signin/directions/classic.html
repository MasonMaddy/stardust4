Direction · Playground sign-in

01 · Classic

WIP Updated 2026-06-21 DRI: Sam

The Classic direction is a centred, neutral sign-in treatment — brand emblem, centred heading, stacked fields, and a full-width primary action on a plain surface. It is the lowest-risk, most conventional of the six directions: the form is the focus, with no decorative hero. Every screen below is a live embed of the prototype (../index.html?v=1&bare=1), so this page always reflects the real build.

3Usage

When to use Classic

  • The default, lowest-risk choice — maximum familiarity, no surprises.
  • When the form should be the focus and you don't want a decorative hero competing for attention.
  • Brand-light contexts — the emblem is present but restrained.
  • Works at any device size without a bespoke landscape composition.

When to consider another direction

  • Editorial (02) — for a left-aligned, more typographic brand moment.
  • Playful tall-scene / card (03 / 04) — when you want warmth and personality (the kid-friendly hero scene).
  • Floating card (05) — a softer, contained card on a tinted surface.
  • Immersive teal (06) — a full-bleed branded entry with inverted controls.
