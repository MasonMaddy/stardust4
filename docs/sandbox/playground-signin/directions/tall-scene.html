Playful tall-scene

WIP Updated 2026-06-23 · v0.2 DRI: Sam

The Playful tall-scene is the chosen direction for the Playground educator sign-in. A full-bleed teal hero (a gentle, kid-friendly drifting scene) sits over a white sheet, on real Stardust --sd-* tokens — warm and welcoming for an OSHC / childcare audience. The hero brand mark swaps with progress: Playground “P” → the service’s logo → the signing-in educator’s photo. Designed for a shared room tablet, so Switch educator, Log out, and an idle auto-lock are first-class. Every screen below is a live embed of the prototype (../version-0.4/index.html?…&bare=1), so this page always reflects the real build.

    3Usage

    Why tall-scene

    • Warm, kid-friendly entry that suits the OSHC / childcare audience without being childish.
    • The swapping brand mark reinforces who is signing in and which service at each step.
    • Built for a shared room tablet: Switch educator, Log out, and an idle auto-lock keep the next person from acting as the signed-in educator.
    • The sliding hero + sheet gives the flow a single, continuous motion rather than hard screen cuts.

    Watch-outs

    • The hero scene + photos lean on imagery — keep contrast on the white text over teal and provide image fallbacks (initials / letter tiles, already handled).
    • Decorative motion is prefers-reduced-motion-guarded; don’t add motion that can’t be frozen.
    • Educator photos / service logos in the prototype are placeholders — swap for real assets before release.
