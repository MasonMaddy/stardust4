# Discovery backlog card template (Jira)

A discovery backlog card holds an opportunity whose research is **not yet complete**. It captures
what's known, what's missing, and the discovery work outstanding — and fills out over time.

## Placeholder fields (to be replaced with the real format)

- **Summary** — the opportunity in one line.
- **Problem / opportunity** — what we think is true and who it affects.
- **Why now** — the trigger.
- **What we know** — current evidence (link the research report).
- **What we don't know yet** — the open-questions checklist (from research report §7).
- **Discovery tasks** — interviews to run, data to pull, flows/maps to draw.
- **Definition of "discovery done"** — what must be answered before a `product-brief` is justified.
- **Links** — research report page, related Canny posts, related epics.

## Lifecycle

1. Created from `product-research` when scope is research-incomplete.
2. Updated over time (re-run the skill to append findings, maps, interview notes).
3. When "discovery done" is met → hand off to `product-brief`.
